Key findings:
- Tight SL + wide TP = profitable (3/7 best)
- Wide SL = trap (higher WR but bleed more per loss)
- Too tight SL (2%) = stopped out too often
- Too wide TP (10%) = never hits, trades stay open
- 3% SL / 7% TP is the sweet spot for ETH 5min
